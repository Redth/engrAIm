---
description: Run a guarded retrospective — consolidate recent learnings, resolve verdicts, prune stale overrides.
---
Periodically review and tighten the workspace's memory. Follow the `engraim-retro` skill, which enforces two guardrails (echo-chamber test and action test). In short:
1. Review active overrides (`list_overrides`), pending/resolved verdicts (`pending_verdicts`), recently staged sessions, and low-confidence or conflicting facts.
2. Consolidate: merge duplicative wiki pages, supersede outdated facts, turn resolved negative verdicts into `calibrate` rules, and propose promotion of well-worn standing overrides.
3. Prune: let stale overrides expire (don't reaffirm what no longer applies); note removed sources in `registry.md`.
4. Apply the guardrails before keeping anything (see the skill), then report a short summary of what changed and what you deliberately chose NOT to keep.
