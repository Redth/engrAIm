---
description: Scan the wiki for gaps and turn them into a research agenda.
---
Run the `engraim-memory` tool `lint_wiki`. It writes a prioritized agenda to `wiki/_gaps.md`: dangling `[[links]]` (concepts referenced but unwritten — highest value), stub pages, orphans, and under-linked pages. Present the top gaps. Then, following the `engraim-research` skill, offer to research and write the most useful ones (prefer gaps that would change a future action; skip trivia). Re-run after writing to confirm the agenda shrank.
