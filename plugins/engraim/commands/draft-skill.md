Turn a recurring procedure into a draft skill. Follow the `engraim-skillsmith` skill. In short: identify the repeatable recipe (from this session or from curated history), author a tight SKILL.md (kebab-case name, third-person trigger description, focused body, optional scripts), and call `draft_skill`. The draft lands in `.engraim/pending/skills/` and stays inert until you `/engraim:promote-skill` it. If Anthropic's skill-creator is installed, prefer authoring/evaluating there first, then draft the result here to enter EngrAIm's gate.
